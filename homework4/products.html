Our Toys

Counting Sticks

Counting sticks can be used to teach children how to count and other math skills. The children can learn addition, substraction, multiplication, division, and comparisons through tiles, sticks, or both.

Hot Potato

Plush electronic potato that plays music for a certain amount of time. Begin the music and pass the potato around, but don't get caught holding the potato when the music stops. Young children can use the potato to play a game of hot potato, and learn hand-eye coordination and catching.

Lego

Legos are building blocks of different colors, shapes, and sizes. Legos can be used to help kids use their imagination, and promote creativity. They can also be used to teach kids to follow instructions.

ABC Blocks

ABC blocks are blocks with Capital letters and numbers on each blocks. These blocks can be used for stacking, and teaching the alphabet, spelling and numbers.

Play Dough

Play doh is non-toxic reusable modelling clay used for art and craft projects like scupting. Play doh can be used to help kids practice letters, shapes, and numbers, improve fine motor skills, use their imagination, and promote creativity.
